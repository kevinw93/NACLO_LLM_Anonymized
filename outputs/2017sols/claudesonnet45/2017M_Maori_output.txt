1. **Regular active sentences:** [Tense marker] [Subject] [Verb] [i] [Object] [ki location]

- Kua = perfective (completed action)
- I = past
- Kāore... i = past negative
- Kāore... e = future negative

Passive forms often involve adding suffixes like -ia, -a, -tia to verbs.
